Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our pickup & delivery laundry service in Penang

Which areas do you provide pickup service?

We provide pickup service from Batu Ferringhi to the Gurney area. For our valued business clients, we extend our service coverage all the way to Georgetown and surrounding areas!

How much does pickup and delivery cost?

Pickup and delivery costs RM5 per order. However, we offer complimentary pickup and delivery for our regular business clients.

What's the turnaround time for laundry service?

Standard wash & fold service takes 24-48 hours for optimal care and quality. Same-day service is available for an additional RM10 per kg (applies to wash & fold and ironing services).
